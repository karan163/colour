Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our trading challenges

How does the challenge work?

+

Our trading challenge is a multi-phase evaluation process designed to identify skilled traders. You start with a simulated account and must meet specific profit targets while adhering to risk management rules. The Rapid Start is a single phase, Pro Trader has two phases, and Elite Challenge has three phases. Upon successful completion, you'll receive a funded account with real capital.

When do I get funded?

+

You receive funding after successfully completing all phases of your chosen challenge. For Rapid Start, this means achieving the profit target in one phase. Pro Trader requires completion of two phases, and Elite Challenge requires three phases. Once you pass all phases, you'll receive access to a funded account with the same balance as your challenge account.

What is the refund policy?

+

The refund policy varies by plan. Rapid Start challenges do not offer refunds. However, Pro Trader and Elite Challenge participants receive a full refund of their challenge fee with their first profit payout from the funded account. This means your initial investment is returned once you start earning profits as a funded trader.

Can I use EAs (Expert Advisors)?

+

No, Expert Advisors (EAs) and automated trading systems are not allowed during the evaluation phases or on funded accounts. We require all trading to be done manually to assess your personal trading skills and decision-making abilities. Copy trading and third-party signals are also prohibited for the same reason.

Do you allow news trading?

+

Yes, news trading is fully allowed on all our challenges and funded accounts. You can trade during high-impact news events, economic releases, and market announcements. We believe that skilled traders should be able to capitalize on all market opportunities, including those created by fundamental news events.

How do payouts work?

+

Payouts are processed bi-weekly (every 14 days) for funded traders who have made profits. You keep 80% of the profits while we retain 20%. Payouts are made via bank transfer, PayPal, or cryptocurrency, depending on your preference and location. The minimum payout amount is $100, and there are no hidden fees or charges on withdrawals.

What is the profit split?

+

Our profit split is 80/20 in your favor. This means you keep 80% of all profits generated on your funded account, while PropFirm retains 20%. This split remains consistent throughout your time as a funded trader. There are no scaling tiers or changing percentages - it's a straightforward 80/20 split from your first payout onwards.

What platforms do you support?

+

We support both MetaTrader 4 (MT4) and MetaTrader 5 (MT5) platforms. You can choose your preferred platform when purchasing a challenge. Both platforms offer full functionality including all major currency pairs, commodities, indices, and cryptocurrencies. Our servers provide fast execution and minimal slippage for optimal trading conditions.
